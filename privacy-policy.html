Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 14, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to GeoPin ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience while using our geography quiz application.

2. Information We Collect

GeoPin collects minimal information to provide you with the best experience:

  • Game Progress: Your scores, completed categories, and game statistics are stored locally on your device.
  • Premium Purchase Information: When you purchase premium content, we process payment information through Apple's App Store. We do not store your payment details.
  • Language Preference: Your selected language (English or Turkish) is stored locally.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain the GeoPin service
  • Track your game progress and achievements
  • Enable premium features after purchase
  • Improve user experience and app functionality

4. Data Storage

All game data is stored locally on your device. We do not transmit your personal game data to external servers. Premium purchase verification is handled securely through Apple's App Store infrastructure.

5. Third-Party Services

GeoPin uses the following third-party services:

  • RevenueCat: For managing in-app purchases and premium subscriptions
  • OpenStreetMap & CartoDB: For providing map tiles and geographic data

6. Children's Privacy

GeoPin is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The app does not require account creation or personal information submission.

7. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your locally stored game data
  • Delete the app and all associated data from your device
  • Request information about your premium purchases through Apple

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
